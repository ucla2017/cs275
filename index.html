Introduction

This paper presents a method to teach the bird how to fly among objects without collision in a game. The game is based on a very popular mobile game called Flappy Bird. We implemeted it in 3D with WebGL, making it easy for access with browsers. The bird has two different perception models, with fovy, or without fovy but has shorttime memory. In both models, the perception state is represented by 16-element array, consisting of coordinates of two pillars in front of the bird. Then we used reinforcement learning to teach the bird how to make correct actions according to current perception state. The results show that the model with fovy has much shorter traning time, while the other model enbales the bird to fly over a longer distance.
